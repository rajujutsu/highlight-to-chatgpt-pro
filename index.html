FAQ

Quick answers.

Does it track my browsing?

No. Courier Web does not run analytics or track browsing behavior. It only acts when you trigger it.

What data is stored?

Templates and history can be stored locally in your browser. See the Privacy page for specifics.

Do I need ChatGPT?

You need an AI tool account to generate responses. Courier Web helps you send context quickly.

How does Pro licensing work?

Pro unlocks advanced templates/favorites and more. Device limits and resets are explained on Support.
