# Highlight → Ask ChatGPT Pro — Support Thanks for using Highlight → Ask ChatGPT Pro. This page covers the most common questions: **lost keys**, **new devices**, **device limit**, and **basic troubleshooting**. --- ## 🔑 Lost your license key? (Key recovery) Your license key is included in your **Gumroad receipt email** sent at purchase. ### Try this first (fastest) 1. Search your inbox for: **Gumroad** 2. Also search: **Highlight → Ask ChatGPT Pro** 3. Check **Spam / Promotions / Social** folders 4. Make sure you’re checking the **same email used at checkout** - If you paid with PayPal, your PayPal email may be different. ### Still can’t find it? Open a support request (GitHub Issue) and include: - The **email used at checkout** - Approximate **purchase date** - Product name: **Highlight → Ask ChatGPT Pro** We’ll help you recover it. --- ## 💻 Device limit / new computer (2 devices per license) Each Pro license can be used on **up to 2 devices**. You might hit the device limit if: - You installed on multiple computers - You reinstalled your browser/OS and it generated a new device ID ### If you got a new device Use **Reset devices** (see below), then activate again on the new device. --- ## 🔄 Reset devices (with cooldown) If you hit the device limit, you can reset your device slots. ✅ **Reset devices is available once every 30 days** (cooldown). ### How it works - Reset devices clears the saved device list for your license - After resetting, you can activate again on up to **2 devices** - Cooldown prevents abuse (so the licensing stays fair) ### If Reset says “cooldown” It means you’ve reset recently. The message should show your next reset date/time. --- ## ✅ Activation checklist (when Pro won’t unlock) If activation fails: 1. Make sure the key is pasted **exactly** (no extra spaces) 2. Confirm you’re online 3. Try again after a minute (rate limit protection) 4. If you see **Device limit reached**, use **Reset devices** 5. If you still can’t activate, contact support (below) --- ## 🛠 Troubleshooting ### “Pro turned off” after reinstall / new browser profile That’s normal. Your browser storage may have been cleared. - Just **activate again** with your key. ### Shortcuts / floating buttons not showing - Confirm the extension is enabled - Reload the page - Make sure you highlighted enough text (minimum selection setting) --- ## 📩 Contact / Support Open a GitHub Issue and include: - What happened (steps) - Browser + OS - Any error message shown - (Optional) Screenshot of the error > Never post your full license key publicly. > If you need to share it, redact most of it (example: `ABCD-****-****-WXYZ`).
