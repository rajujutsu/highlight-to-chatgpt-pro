Lost your license key?

1) Check your email receipt
  • Search your inbox for: Gumroad + Highlight → Ask ChatGPT Pro
  • Open the receipt — your license key is inside
  • Common subjects: “You bought …” or “Receipt from Gumroad”
2) Didn’t get the email?
  • Try the email used at checkout (PayPal email may differ)
  • Check Promotions and Spam folders
3) Still stuck?
  • Contact support with:
    • Email used
    • Purchase date
    • Last 4 digits (card) or PayPal transaction ID
This avoids sending you to any sketchy “mystery login” pages.
Common fixes:
  • If templates or favorites don’t appear immediately, close the menu and right-click again (Chrome menus don’t refresh mid-open).
  • If you reinstalled, use Restore on the Options page to re-activate Pro.
