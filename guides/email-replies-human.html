Prompt Engineering → Email Replies (Human)

Email replies that don’t sound like AI

The secret is not “more polite.” It’s clear intent, tone rules, and realistic structure. This template turns highlights into usable replies.

Use this when

You highlight an email or message and want a reply that feels calm, professional, and human.

Copy-ready reply template (paste as a button)

ROLE: You are a professional communicator. TASK: Write a reply to the highlighted message. INTENT: - Goal: (what we want to happen next) - Boundary: (what we cannot/will not do) TONE: - Respectful, warm, concise - No buzzwords, no "As an AI" - No excessive exclamation points CONSTRAINTS: - If info is missing, ask up to 2 clarifying questions at the end. - Keep the reply under 170 words unless necessary. OUTPUT FORMAT: Subject: (optional) Reply: - Greeting - 2–5 short paragraphs - Clear next step - Sign-off HIGHLIGHT: {HIGHLIGHT}

Variant: customer support

INTENT: - Goal: acknowledge issue + give next step - Boundary: don’t promise dates you can’t guarantee Add to CONSTRAINTS: - Apologize once. Then move to action. - Include 1 concrete next step.

Variant: sales / partnership

INTENT: - Goal: book a call or move to a clear next action - Boundary: stay concise Add to OUTPUT FORMAT: - 2 bullet options for next steps - 2 time windows for scheduling

Pro tip: “sound like me” line

This line is gold when you want consistency:

Write in my voice: calm, direct, helpful. Short sentences. No fluff. Avoid corporate words.
